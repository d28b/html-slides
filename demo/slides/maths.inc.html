At each time step, the ball position \(p\) is updated as follows:

$$ p_{n+1} = p_{n} + v_n $$

The velocity \(v\) undergoes friction \(f\) and gravity \(g\):

$$ v_{n+1} = f v_{n} + \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ g \end{array} \right) $$

For this simulation, we chose:

$$ f = 0.995 $$

$$ g = -0.1 $$
